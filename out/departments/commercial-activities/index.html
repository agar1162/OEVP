Commercial Activities works closely on both ongoing and future partnerships with the ASUC and UC Berkeley students. Our department ensures that important platforms, such as the Wall Street Journal and the New York Times, remain accessible to students. Beyond maintaining these existing relationships, we also explore and establish new partnerships with platforms that are heavily used by students, broadening the range of resources available to the campus community....
